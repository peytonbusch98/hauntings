Simpson Ghost Hunters

The Simpson Ghost Hunters investigate haunted locations and paranormal activity around the Simpson College campus. These experiences are real and can be backed up with witnesses and evidence. Our hunters have spent hours on lockdown inside these locations and are trained to defend themselves from the paranormal. Due to the unpredictable nature of these anomalous creatures, we highly advise that you never try this alone.

Recources for Ghost Hunters

Need help getting started? Or just want more information?

Dunn Library

A great place to get started with your ghosthunting is our own library. They have plenty of books, magezines, and movies to get you started with your new hobby. Stuck? Ask a librarian. They may give you a funny look, but it's worth it.

George

If you've ever been to Simpson's campus, you are aware of George the cat. He may just seem like an ordinary cat. However, George is a great recource for ghosthunters. He's smart, intelligent, and handsome.

Internet

If all else fails you, the internet is a great recourse! You can find anything from hauntings in your area, to tips and tricks, or anything you would like.
